Our Story

Welcome to Restoran

Founded in 2008 by acclaimed chef Alessandra Monteiro, Restoran began as an intimate bistro dedicated to showcasing the finest local ingredients through innovative culinary techniques. What started as a passion project quickly evolved into one of the city's most celebrated dining destinations.

Our philosophy centers on honoring traditional flavors while embracing contemporary approaches. We work directly with local farmers, fishermen, and artisanal producers to ensure every ingredient that enters our kitchen meets our exacting standards for quality, sustainability, and ethical sourcing.

Over the years, we've expanded our vision while staying true to our core values. Today, Restoran is proud to offer an unparalleled dining experience that combines culinary excellence with warm, attentive service in an elegant yet comfortable atmosphere.
